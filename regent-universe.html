About The Universe

Classes Regent Universe is more than just a website: it’s the ultimate interactive learning experience for Regent students. This is Regent’s latest innovation from the Applied Innovation Department. Showcasing blogs, vlogs, podcasting, shows & more, Regent Universe is an opportunity for students to apply their skills & innovate their learning to liveauthentic audiences. From productions to showcases to student leadership to interactive competitions, Regent Universe allows students, teachers & the wider school community to actively engage education simultaneously: physically in class and whilst collaborating online, across campus or across the world! The Regent community is borderless!



Regent Universe provides teachers with 21st C tools to provide innovative learning experiences. It gives students future-proofed industry 4.0 technologies & opportunities to lead, shape & develop their education, skills & learning. #CreatingFuture The Regent Universe;s primary purpose is to teach students through empowerment; they learn to solve problems & think critically as they harness both traditional & 21st Century social, authoring & media & coding technologies to celebrate & connect, to publish & engage and to uplift & inspire. For those daring coders, the Regent Galaxy offers students the chance to create & hard-code from scratch! We’re #FutureProofing out students because… #FutureIsNow
